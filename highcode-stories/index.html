01 / FLOW

Three steps to a populated backlog.

STEP_01 / UPLOAD

Drop a document.

PDF, Word or Markdown. Drag onto the dropzone or browse. DOCX is converted to text in your browser before it leaves.

STEP_02 / EXTRACT

AI does the parsing.

An n8n + LLM pipeline reads the doc, identifies epics and stories, and writes summaries you would not be ashamed to ship.

STEP_03 / SHIP

Issues hit Jira.

Tickets land in the RN project, linked back to source. History page tracks every run, with deep links to each issue created.
